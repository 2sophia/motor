## How attribution works

- **Commits** show the author the work was done as. The motor's
  history mixes all four — `git log --pretty=format:"%an"` shows
  who-did-what without ambiguity.
- **GitHub Releases** are published by the active `sophia-ai-dev`
  account — that's the brand voice of the product.
- **Co-Authored-By** trailers preserve the pair-coding pattern. Every
  feature commit ends with the model that helped author it.
